🎯 What is SenseAI?

SenseAI is an AI-powered institutional knowledge platform installed directly on your school's server. It uses Large Language Models (LLMs) running entirely offline to help teachers, students, and administrators work smarter — without sharing any data with external cloud services.

🧩 Core Modules
📚

Knowledge Assistant

Ask questions in plain English. Get accurate answers sourced from your school's uploaded documents — syllabus, circulars, handbooks, policies.

✍️

Auto Question Generator

Generate CBSE-aligned MCQ, Short Answer, and Essay questions from any chapter. Review, approve, and build your question bank in minutes.

📝

Student Test Module

Publish MCQ tests to students. Auto-graded results with per-question analytics and CSV reports. Ready for CBSE, NEET, JEE Main & JEE Advanced preparation.

🎮

Live Quiz & Student Sessions

Run real-time classroom quizzes with a live leaderboard. Students join via a 6-digit PIN — no accounts or login required. Results saved automatically.

🔒 Your Data. Your School. Your Server.

Unlike cloud AI tools, SenseAI runs 100% on-premise. No student names, exam data, or documents are sent to any external server. All AI processing happens locally using Llama 3.1 — one of the world's most advanced open-source language models.

📚 Module 1 — Institutional Knowledge Assistant
🔍

Smart Document Search

Upload PDFs, Word docs, and images. SenseAI indexes them with AI embeddings and answers questions with exact source references.

🗂️

Subject-wise Organization

Documents organized by subject and domain. Each teacher sees only relevant content. No information overload.

💬

Natural Language Q&A

Ask "What is the attendance policy?" or "Explain Newton's second law from Chapter 5" — get precise, grounded answers.

🛡️

Hallucination Detection

Built-in confidence scoring flags low-reliability answers. Admins see hallucination counts on the dashboard.

✍️ Module 2 — Auto Question Generator (AQG)
🎯

CBSE / NEET / JEE Question Patterns

Generates MCQ (1 mark), Short Answer, and Essay questions aligned to CBSE, NEET, JEE Main, and JEE Advanced patterns — covering all difficulty levels.

🔄

Bulk Generation

Generate 10, 20, or 50 questions in one job. AI processes chapter content and produces a full question bank automatically.

✅

Tenant Admin Review Workflow

Tenant Admin approves or rejects individual questions. Bulk approve/reject with one click. Only approved questions go to tests.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track questions generated, approved, and rejected per subject. Monitor AQG job history and performance from the admin dashboard.

📝 Module 3 — Student Test Module
🏫

Publish Tests to Students

Teacher selects approved MCQ questions and publishes a timed test. Students access it from their own devices on the school LAN.

⚡

Instant Auto-Grading

Student submits test → marks calculated instantly. No manual checking. Teacher sees per-student scores in real time.

📈

Per-Question Analytics

See which questions students struggled with. Difficulty analysis helps teachers focus revision on weak areas.

⬇️

CSV Export Reports

Download student-wise results as CSV. Import into Excel for school records, parent communication, or progress reports.

🔑

PIN-Based Student Sessions

Teacher generates a 6-digit PIN and shares it with the class. Students join instantly from any device — no account or login required.

🏆

Live Quiz with Leaderboard

Run a real-time classroom quiz — teacher controls the pace, one question at a time. Live leaderboard keeps students engaged and competitive.
