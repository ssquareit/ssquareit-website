What is Sense AI? — An offline AI-powered Knowledge Assistant and Question Paper Generator for schools and colleges. Runs 100% on your school's own hardware. No internet needed after installation. No data leaves your premises.
1. Server Machine Requirements (One Dedicated PC)
Component Minimum Recommended
Operating System Windows 10 (64-bit) Windows 11 (64-bit)
RAM 16 GB 32 GB
Processor Intel Core i5 / AMD Ryzen 5 Intel Core i7 / AMD Ryzen 7 (8 cores)
Storage 60 GB free disk space 120 GB free SSD
Usage Must be a dedicated machine — not used for other heavy software simultaneously
Note: The server machine does not need to be a rack server. A regular desktop PC or laptop meeting the above specs works fine. It should remain switched on during school hours so students can access the AI assistant.

3. Student & Teacher Devices
Item Requirement Notes

4. Pre-Installation Checklist

Please confirm the following before our team's visit:

  • Dedicated Windows 10/11 PC is available
  • PC has at least 16 GB RAM
  • PC has at least 60 GB free storage
  • PC is connected to school's WiFi / LAN
  • Internet available on the day of installation
  • Windows admin login credentials available
  • School's WiFi password is available
  • At least one teacher device for testing
